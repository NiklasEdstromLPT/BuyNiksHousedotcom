1. Executive Summary & Product Vision 
 
BuyMyHouse.com is a seller origination and dual-path conversion platform operated by LPT 
Holdings, Inc. It is not a standard real estate search site. It is a meticulously engineered multi-
stage psychological conversion architecture designed to capture homeowners at the precise 
moment of financial decision-making  upstream of MLS listings  and move them toward a 
no-cost, in-person Professional Property Assessment. 
 
  CRITICAL REFRAME: This is a SELLER funnel, not a buyer search product. 
   The user is a homeowner who may want to sell. 
   The product's job is to move them from curious  committed  assessed. 
   There are no property listings. There are no buyer-facing features. 
   Every UI decision advances the seller through 7 psychological states. 
 
The Dual-Path Offer System 
The funnel culminates in a Professional Property Assessment where the seller is presented with 
two executable options: 
 The Certainty Path: A firm cash offer  speed, certainty, no repairs, no showings. 
 The Market Path: A traditional listing with a licensed local LPT Realtor  maximum market 
value. 
 
Both paths are real. The platforms role is to ensure the seller arrives at the assessment in a 
high-intent, psychologically committed state. 
 
Company Context (from buymyhouse des.pdf) 
LPT Holdings operates a Four-Layer Integrated Business Model. BuyMyHouse.com powers 
Layer 1: 
 
Layer Name What It Does 
1   
BuyMyHouse.com

2 Transaction Routing Brokerage control plane with 20,000 managed professionals 
3 Agent Products CRM, lead gen, tech tools sold as SaaS to agent network 
4 Placement Layer Workforce infrastructure   recruits and allocates agents 
 
Hackathon Focus: Pod 1 owns everything from the landing page through 
Confirmation. Layers 24 are downstream and out of scope.
